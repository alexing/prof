Kanboard is a project management software that use the Kanban methodology

Version 1.0.18 released (August 30, 2015)

Kanboard is not for everybody, it's made for people who want to manage their projects efficiently and simply.

  • Kanban methodology
  • Visualize your work
  • Limit your work in progress to be more efficient
  • Customize your boards according to your business activities
  • Multiple boards with the ability to drag and drop tasks
  • Reports and analytics
  • Fast and simple to use
  • Access from anywhere with a modern browser
  • Free, open source and self-hosted
  • Super simple installation
  • No fancy technologies, we don't care about the last hipster framework
